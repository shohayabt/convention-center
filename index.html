ABOUT US

SST Convention Centre is one of the ORIGINAL and LEADING convention centers in Bangladesh. It was established in 2015 with the goal of providing the highest standard of services in the Hospitality Industry. SST Convention Centre offers Luxurious Banquet Halls, Business Conference Rooms, Business Centre, all of which that are equipped with a modern hygienic Kitchen, car parking facilities and a fantastic and cozy location in the central business district of Gulshan, Dhaka.
